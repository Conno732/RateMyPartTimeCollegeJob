About the Project

Rate Your University Job is a discussion board that allows students to review their work-study jobs, and internships. Do you want to pick up some convenient work over the summer or make some cash during your free time? Are you unsure of which jobs you could be applying tor? Head to Rate Your University Job and see how other students rate different part-time jobs around you.

Rate Your University Job was created for the Yhack 2022 Virtual Hackathon.
